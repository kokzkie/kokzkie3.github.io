Top 10 Tips from Nurse Dominic

  • 💧 Stay hydrated — drink 8+ cups of water daily.
  • 🥗 Eat a balanced diet rich in veggies, fruits, and whole grains.
  • 🚶 Move your body — daily physical activity keeps you strong.
  • 😴 Get 7–9 hours of quality sleep every night.
  • 🧘‍♀️ Manage stress with mindfulness, deep breathing, or journaling.
  • 🧴 Wash hands regularly to prevent infections.
  • 🩺 Keep up with checkups and vaccinations.
  • 🚭 Avoid smoking and limit alcohol use.
  • 🌞 Get safe sun exposure for Vitamin D.
  • 😊 Nurture your mental health — it's just as important!
